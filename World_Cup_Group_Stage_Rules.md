# World Cup Group Stage
### Monday League Contest — Holes 1–9

## How to Play

Every hole is its own "match." Your net score (gross score minus any handicap strokes you receive on that hole) is compared to par.

| Result | Points |
|---|---|
| Net birdie or better | 3 (Win) |
| Net par | 1 (Draw) |
| Net bogey or worse | 0 (Loss) |

Add up your points across holes 1–9. Most points wins the group.

## Tiebreaker: Penalty Shootout

If two or more players finish tied on total points, across all 9 holes:

1. **Most holes won** (net birdie or better = 3 pts). Most wins takes the tie.
2. **Still tied?** Most holes drawn (net par = 1 pt) takes the tie.

The idea: whoever beat par most often gets the advantage.

## Sudden Death

If still tied after the Penalty Shootout:

1. Apply the same test — most holes won, then most holes drawn — starting from just **Hole 9**.
2. **Still tied?** Widen the window backward one hole at a time (Holes 8–9, then 7–9, and so on) until someone separates.

---
*Handicap strokes per hole are determined by each player's course handicap and the hole's stroke allocation index.*
